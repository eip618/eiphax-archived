A guaranteed solution to your problems

This short guide is intended to offer a guaranteed solution to any problem you may be having with your console or device.
It is failsafe, foolproof and works 100% of the time.

Here is the solution:

  1. Stop doing it.

It may sound counterproductive, but if you are having a problem trying to do something, no longer trying to do it will solve your problem. Here are a few examples.

  • If you are having trouble installing CFW on your console, stop trying to install CFW.
  • If you are finding it difficult to repair a broken item, stop trying to repair it.
  • If it is hard for you to reverse parallel park, stop trying to reverse parallel park.

I think by now you understand how easy it can be to solve any problem you have. Thank you for visiting eipHax.
